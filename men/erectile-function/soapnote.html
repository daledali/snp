International Index of Erectile Function Questionnaire
SUMMARY: Questionnaire for assessing impotence/erectile dysfunction.
Questions: Based on last 6 months (each answer assigned 1-5 score)
<-- How do you rate your confidence that you could get and keep an Erection?
<-- When you had Erections with sexual stimulation, how often were your Erections hard enough for penetration?
<-- During sexual intercourse, how often were you able to maintain your Erection after you had penetrated or entered your partner?
<-- During sexual intercourse, how difficult was it maintain your Erection to completion of intercourse?
<-- When you attempted sexual intercourse, how often was it satisfactory for you?
Score --> totalscore=(Q1)+(Q2)+(Q3)+(Q4)+(Q5) points
Interpretation --> Interpretationscore2=(Q1)+(Q2)+(Q3)+(Q4)+(Q5);score2>21?'Normal erectile function':score2>16?'Mild erectile dysfunction':score2>11?'Mild to moderate erectile dysfunction':score2>7?'Moderate erectile dysfunction':'Severe erectile dysfunction'
