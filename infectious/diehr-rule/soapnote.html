Diehr Rule to Diagnose Pneumonia in Adults
<-- Rhinorrhea
<-- Sore throat
<-- Myalgia
<-- Night Sweats
<-- Sputum produced throughout the day
<-- Respiratory rate over 25 breaths per minute
<-- Temperature over 100 degrees F or 37.8 C
