Thyroiditis Decision Tool
Summary: How to proceed when thyroiditis is suspected.
<-- Thyroid Pain
<-- Taking amiodarone, interferon alfa, interleukin-2, or kinase inhibitor
<-- TSH
DIAGNOSIS --> Drug-induced thyroiditis.
<-- Postpartum
DIAGNOSIS --> Hashimoto or silent thyroiditis of subacute thyroiditis (hypothyroid phase).
DIAGNOSIS --> Postpartum or Hashimoto thyroiditis (hypothyroid phase).
<-- Thyroid scan with radioactive iodine update
DIAGNOSIS --> Silent or subacute thyroiditis (hyperthyroid phase).
DIAGNOSIS --> Graves disease.
DIAGNOSIS --> Postpartum thyroiditis (hyperthyroid phase).
<-- Toxic-appearing patient.
DIAGNOSIS --> Infectious thyroiditis.
<-- History of Radiation or Trauma
DIAGNOSIS --> Radiation- or trauma-induced thyroiditis
<-- Low TSH and High Thyroglobulin Level
DIAGNOSIS --> Thyroid hemorrhage
DIAGNOSIS --> Subacute thyroiditis
