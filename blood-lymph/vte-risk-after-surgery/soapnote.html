Venous Thromboembolism Risk after Surgery
<-- Age at least 60 years (1 point)
<-- BMI at least 40 kg/m2 (1 point)
<-- Gender (male = 2 points, female = 0 points)
<-- Sepsis (3 points)
<-- Septic shock (3 points)
<-- Systemic Inflammatory Response Syndrome (3 points)
<-- Personal history of venous thromboembolism (3 points)
<-- Family history of venous thromboembolism (4 points)
<-- Current cancer (5 points)

Score --> numberscore1=(age)+(bmi)+(sex)+(sepsis)+(shock)+(sirs)+(vtehx)+(vtefam)+(cancer)
Interpretation --> resultscore2=(age)+(bmi)+(sex)+(sepsis)+(shock)+(sirs)+(vtehx)+(vtefam)+(cancer);score2>5?'High risk, 3.2 percent':score2>2?'Moderate risk, 1.8 percent':'Very low risk, 0.4 percent'
