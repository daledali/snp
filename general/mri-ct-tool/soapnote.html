MRI/CT Scan Decision Tool
Summary: This tool provides suggestions for Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI) or Computed Tomography (CT) studies based on body area and clinical condition. Other studies, including ultrasound exist and may be more appropriate. It is always wise to consult with radiology regarding the proper study to order.
