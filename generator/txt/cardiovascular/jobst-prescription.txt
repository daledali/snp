#1 Lower extremity edema
Compression stockings are prescribed to help mobilize fluid and improve orthostasis.
A prescription is given for ***20-30 mm Hg Knee High Compression Stocking/20-30 mm Hg Thigh High Compression Stocking/30-40mm Hg Knee High Compression Stocking/30-40mm Hg Thigh High Compression Stocking***.  Quantity 1. No refills. Sig: Apply to legs each am off each pm at hs.
