Hyperlipidemia
Begin ***Statin***
Pt will need a recheck lipids and ALT/AST in 12 weeks.  Goal lipid levels are as follows: Total cholesterol less than 170, triglycerides less than 150, HDL greater than 40, and an LDL less than 100. Will titrate statin therapy to obtain goal lipid levels.
