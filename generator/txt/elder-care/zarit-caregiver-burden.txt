The Zarit Caregiver Burden Interview

[select name="Q2" value="Never (0 points)=0|Rarely (1 point)=1|Sometimes (2 points)=2|Quite Frequently (3 points)=3|Nearly Always (4 points)=4"] <--Do you feel stressed between caring for this person and trying to meet other responsibilities (work, family)?
[select name="Q3" value="Never (0 points)=0|Rarely (1 point)=1|Sometimes (2 points)=2|Quite Frequently (3 points)=3|Nearly Always (4 points)=4"] <--Do you feel angry when you are around this person?
[select name="Q4" value="Never (0 points)=0|Rarely (1 point)=1|Sometimes (2 points)=2|Quite Frequently (3 points)=3|Nearly Always (4 points)=4"] <--Do you feel that this person currently affects your relationships with family members or friends in a negative way?
[select name="Q5" value="Never (0 points)=0|Rarely (1 point)=1|Sometimes (2 points)=2|Quite Frequently (3 points)=3|Nearly Always (4 points)=4"] <--Do you feel strained when you are around this person?
[select name="Q6" value="Never (0 points)=0|Rarely (1 point)=1|Sometimes (2 points)=2|Quite Frequently (3 points)=3|Nearly Always (4 points)=4"] <--Do you feel that your health has suffered because of your involvement with this person?
[select name="Q7" value="Never (0 points)=0|Rarely (1 point)=1|Sometimes (2 points)=2|Quite Frequently (3 points)=3|Nearly Always (4 points)=4"] <--Do you feel that you don't have as much privacy as you would like because of this person?
[select name="Q8" value="Never (0 points)=0|Rarely (1 point)=1|Sometimes (2 points)=2|Quite Frequently (3 points)=3|Nearly Always (4 points)=4"] <--Do you feel that your social life has suffered because you are caring for this person?
[select name="Q9" value="Never (0 points)=0|Rarely (1 point)=1|Sometimes (2 points)=2|Quite Frequently (3 points)=3|Nearly Always (4 points)=4"] <--Do you feel that you have lost control of your life since this person's illness began?
[select name="Q10" value="Never (0 points)=0|Rarely (1 point)=1|Sometimes (2 points)=2|Quite Frequently (3 points)=3|Nearly Always (4 points)=4"] <--Do you feel uncertain about what to do about this person?
[select name="Q11" value="Never (0 points)=0|Rarely (1 point)=1|Sometimes (2 points)=2|Quite Frequently (3 points)=3|Nearly Always (4 points)=4"] <--Do you feel you should be doing more for this person?
[select name="Q12" value="Never (0 points)=0|Rarely (1 point)=1|Sometimes (2 points)=2|Quite Frequently (3 points)=3|Nearly Always (4 points)=4"] <--Do you feel you could do a better job in caring for this person?
