#1 ***Croup/Viral URI/Pharyngitis/Epiglottitis***
Mild croup. No prescription medication use currently indicated
croup brochure given and discussed
racemic epinephrine per neb
transferred to ***hospital/emergency room***
Decadron IM
Prelone
symptomatic measures discussed
advised to increase fluids, humidification, breathing cool air
follow up if no improvement or symptoms worsen
follow up in Emergency Room if any respiratory distress
