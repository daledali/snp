Status post gastric bypass surgery
Lab tests for gastric bypass follow up ordered: ***Glucose, Plasma (fasting)/BMP/CMP/25-Hydroxyvitamin D2 and D3/Bone Alkaline Phosphatase/PTH, Intact w/Minerals/Calcium/TSH/CBC without 5 part differential/Vitamin B12/Fe and TIBC/Ferritin/Lipid Screen/Calcium (24hr) (Urine)/Creatinine (24hr) (Urine)***
Referral to an Infusion Therapy Center for an infusion of 1 gram of iron dextran. She will be premedicated as well with Tylenol 1000 mg and Benadryl 50 mg. I will then recheck her ferritin in one month.
