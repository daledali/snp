#1 Skin tag - shave excision
Skin around skin tag was prepped with Betadine, draped with sterile towels, infiltrated w/Bupivicaine 0.5% ml to raise a wheal, and shave excised. Hemostasis achieved with Aluminum hydroxide 25% without difficulty. Bacitracin and bandaid applied. Tolerated well. Negligible blood loss. Skin tag discarded.
