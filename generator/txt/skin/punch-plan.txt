#1 Skin lesion punch biopsy
After obtaining informed consent, the area was prepped and draped in the usual fashion. Anesthesia was obtained with 1% lidocaine with epinephrine. A full thickness punch biopsy was obtained with a 4mm punch. Wound closed with two simple interrupted sutures of 4-0 Ethilon. Sutures out in 4 days. Wound care discussed. Specimen sent for dermatopathology.
