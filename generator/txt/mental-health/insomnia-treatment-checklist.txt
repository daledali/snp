<strong>COGNITIVE THERAPIES</strong>[/html]
[checkbox value="Discuss realistic expectations about sleep requirements.  "][checkbox value="Discuss consequences of insomnia.  "][html]
<strong>SLEEP HYGIENE EDUCATION</strong>[/html]
[checkbox value="Avoid the use of caffeine in the later hours of the day, limiting the amount of overall caffeine consumption.  "][checkbox value="Avoid heavy meals within two hours of bedtime.  "][checkbox value="Minimize intake of fluids after dinner to prevent frequent nighttime urination.  "][checkbox value="Avoid exercise for 90 minutes to four hours before sleep.  "][checkbox value="Ear-plugs can be used if noise is a problem.  "][checkbox value="Exposure to daytime light for at least 30 minutes in the morning.  "][html]
<strong>STIMULUS CONTROL</strong>[/html]
[checkbox value="Eliminate distractions and associate the bedroom only with sleep and sex. "][checkbox value="Reading and television watching should occur in a room other than the bedroom.  "][checkbox value="Go to bed only when tired and leave the room if not asleep within 15 to 20 minutes.  "][checkbox value="Avoid napping and should get out of bed at the same time each day.  "][html]
