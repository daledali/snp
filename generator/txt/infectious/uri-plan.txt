#1 Viral URI
Recommended ***increased fluids/humidity/12-hour decongestant nasal spray twice daily for a maximum of 4 days/1 tsp honey for the cough prn/Tylenol as needed/Ibuprofen as needed/Steam inhalation for sinus pressure***
Recheck if not improved in one week or sooner if worsening.
