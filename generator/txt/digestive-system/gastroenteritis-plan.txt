#1 Acute viral gastroenteritis
Rehydrate with 1 saltine cracker per pint of water taken as rapidly as tolerated until urine is clear again.
Compazine 10 mg up to four times daily as needed for nausea.
If blood in stool, fever, abdominal pain, lightheaded, worsening signs of dehydration, return to clinic.
If not better in 1 week return to clinic.
