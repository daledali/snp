Short Alcohol Withdrawal Scale (SAWS)
QUESTION: How have you felt in the last 24 hours?
<-- Anxious
<-- Feeling confused
<-- Restless
<-- Miserable
<-- Problems with memory
<-- Tremor (shakes)
<-- Nausea
<-- Heart pounding
<-- Sleep disturbance
<-- Sweating
