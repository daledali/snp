CAGE Questionnaire - Alcoholism screening tool
<-- Have you ever felt the need to Cut down on drinking?
<-- Have you ever felt Annoyed by criticism of your drinking?
<-- Have you ever had Guilty feelings about your drinking?
<-- Do you ever take a morning Eye opener (a drink first thing in the morning to steady your nerves or get rid of a hangover)?
Score --> scorescore=(Q1)+(Q2)+(Q3)+(Q4) out of 4. Interpretation --> interpretationscore=(Q1)+(Q2)+(Q3)+(Q4);score>2?'Confirms Alcoholism.':score>1?'Strongly indicative of Alcoholism. A positive response to at least two questions is seen in the majority of patients with alcoholism. ':score>0?'One positive response to any CAGE question suggests the need for closer assessment. ':'NEGATIVE screen for alcoholism.'
