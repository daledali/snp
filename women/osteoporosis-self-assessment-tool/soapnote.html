Osteoporosis Self-Assessment Tool
Summary: Tool for osteoporosis risk assessment.
USPSTF recommends screening for osteoporosis in women 65 years or older, and in younger women whose fracture risk is equal to or greater than that of a 65-year-old white woman with no additional risk factors.
See also: Osteoporosis SCORE estimation

<-- Age (in years)
<-- Weight (in pounds)
Score --> numberscore1=(((((weight)/(2.2))-(age))/5).toFixed(2))
Interpretation --> risk levelscore2=((((weight)/(2.2))-(age))/5);score2>1?'Low Risk of Osteoporosis':score2>-3.001?'Moderate Risk of Osteoporosis':'High Risk of Osteoporosis'
