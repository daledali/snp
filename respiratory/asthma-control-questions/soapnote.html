Generic Asthma Control Calculator
SUMMARY: Free, unvalidated questionnaire that can be used in assessment of asthma symptom control. The questions are from a 1995 NIH Asthma Pocket Guide referenced in the NAEPP guidelines below.
LINKS:
Asthma Severity Decision Tool
Asthma Control Decision Tool

<-- 1. Has your asthma awakened you at night or early morning?
<-- 2. Have you needed more quick-relief bronchodilator medication (inhaled short-acting beta2-agonist) than usual?
<-- 3. Have you needed any urgent medical care for your asthma, such as unscheduled visits to your doctor, an urgent care clinic, or the emergency department?
<-- 4. Are you participating in your usual and desired activities?
<-- 5. If you are measuring your peak flow, has it been below your personal best?

Score --> numberscore1=(Q1)+(Q2)+(Q3)+(Q4)+(Q5) out of 5 (0 = controlled, 1 to 5 = uncontrolled)
Interpretation --> interpretationscore2=(Q1)+(Q2)+(Q3)+(Q4)+(Q5);score2>0?'Uncontrolled':'Controlled'
