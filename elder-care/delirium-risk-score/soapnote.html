Delirium Predictive Risk Score
<-- BUN (mg/dL)
<-- Creatinine (mg/dL)
<-- Barthel Index go to calculator
Score --> numberscore1=((5.5*(bun)/(creatinine))-(4*(barthel))).toFixed(2)
Interpretation --> resultscore2=(5.5*(bun)/(creatinine))-(4*(barthel));score2>240?'High risk for development of delirium, 45 percent':'Not at high risk for development of delirium'
