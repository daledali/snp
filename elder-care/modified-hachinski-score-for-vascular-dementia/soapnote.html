Modified Hachinski Score Calculates the likelihood dementia is due to vascular causes.
Findings:
<-- Abrupt onset
<-- Stepwise progression
<-- Fluctuating course
<-- Nocturnal confusion
<-- Relative preservation of personality
<-- Depression
<-- Somatic complaints
<-- Emotional incontinence
<-- History of hypertension
<-- History of stroke
<-- Focal neurologic signs
<-- Focal neurologic symptoms
