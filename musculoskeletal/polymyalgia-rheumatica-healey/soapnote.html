Polymyalgia Rheumatica Diagnostic Criteria - Healey
Pain: To meet criteria, 2 of the following 3 areas below must have pain
<-- Pain at neck
<-- Pain at shoulders
<-- Pain at pelvic girdle
------------------------------------------------------------------------
<-- Age over 50 years
<-- Pain persisting for at least one month
<-- Morning stiffness lasting more than 1 hour
<-- Rapid response to prednisone (20 mg/day or less)
<-- Erythrocyte sedimentation rate (ESR) > 40 mm/hour
<-- Absence of other diseases capable of causing the musculoskeletal symptoms
