NEXUS C-Spine Rule
Summary: Evaluate for need of C-spine Xray. This applies the NEXUS C-Spine Rule to determine whether Xray is recommended.

<-- Absence of tenderness at the posterior midline of the C-spine
<-- Absence of a focal neurological deficit
<-- Normal level of alertness
<-- No evidence of intoxication
<-- Absence of clinical apparent pain that might distract the patient from the pain of a C-spine injury
