Ottawa ankle and foot rules
<-- Pain in the malleolar zone
<-- Bone tenderness lower 6 cm (2.5 in) of the fibula
<-- Bone tenderness lower 6 cm (2.5 in) of the tibia
<-- Able to ambulate at injury
<-- Able to ambulate in ER for 4 steps

<-- Bone tenderness over the navicular bone
<-- Bone tenderness over the base of the fifth metatarsal
<-- Able to ambulate at injury
<-- Able to ambulate in ER for 4 steps
