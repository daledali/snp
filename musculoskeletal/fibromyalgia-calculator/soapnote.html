Criteria for fibromyalgia are 1) WPI ≥7 and SS scale score ≥5 OR WPI of 3 to 6 and SS scale score ≥9; 2) Symptoms at a similar level of at least 3 months’ duration; and 3) Pain that cannot be attributed to another etiology.
