Gouty Arthritis Diagnostic Rule
Summary: Diagnostic rule for gouty arthritis which doesn't require joint fluid analysis.
 <-- Gender
<-- Previous patient-reported arthritis attack
<-- Onset of pain within 1 day
<-- Joint redness
<-- 1st MTP joint involvement
<-- Hypertension or cardiovascular disease
<-- Serum uric acid level > 5.88 mg/dl (350 mmol/L)
